The features of HTML5 are :

1. Intro of audio and video :
	Audio and video tags are the two major addition to HTML5.It allows developers to embed a video or audio on their website. Support for various codecs like MP3, AAC, and H.264 without requring third-party plugins.

2.Canvas :
	<canvas> element for drawing graphics and animations using Javascript.

3.Scalable Vector Graphics (SVG) :
	Native support for vector graphics through the <svg> element.

4.Local Storage :
	'localstorage' and 'sessionstorage' for storing data on the client side.

5.Responsive Design :
	Media queries for adapting web pages to different devices and screen sizes.
